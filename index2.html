Coronavirus Guidelines

  • Symptom Monitoring: Watch for fever, cough, shortness of breath, sore throat, difficulty breathing, or loss of taste/smell.
  • Self-Isolation: Self-isolate if sick after travel or exposure; seek medical care if symptoms worsen.
  • Protective Measures: Wash hands frequently, avoid touching face, cough into elbow.
  • Travel Restrictions: Self-isolate for 14 days after returning from outside Canada; seek medical care if needed.
  • Emergency Contact: Toronto Public Health (416-392-1250) or a medical practitioner.

Parking Rules

  • Visitor Parking: Visitors must park at the P1 visitor garage level. Do not park under the daycare or recreation center.
  • Prohibited Parking: Ground floor parking near the recreation center is prohibited per fire code.
  • Daycare Parking: Reserved for management, daycare staff, contractors, and delivery drivers. Unauthorized parking will result in tagging and enforcement.
  • Capacity: Parking is first-come, first-served. Arrange alternative parking if the visitor garage is full.

Pet Management

  • No Common Area Mess: Pets must not urinate or defecate in common areas (hallways, stairways).
  • Clean-Up: Residents must clean up after pets immediately. Failure to do so will result in a $250 charge.
  • Reporting: Report any violations to gatehouse security or management.

Utility Conservation and Oil Disposal

  • Energy Conservation: Use high-efficiency bulbs in your unit. Turn off all lights when leaving.
  • Water Conservation: Report any leaky faucets or water-related issues immediately to prevent waste and damage.
  • Oil Disposal: Dispose of cooking oil/grease in the oil collection drum outside the recycling room. Do not dispose of oil/grease in the kitchen sink.
  • Financial Impact: Higher utility consumption increases bills, directly affecting monthly maintenance fees.

Community Guidelines

Easy Rules:

  • Do not leave packages at the gatehouse without management’s approval.
  • No sitting, food, drink, bicycles, or roller blades in the Rainbow Bridge area.
  • No noise or nuisance that disturbs the comfort or quiet enjoyment of others.
  • Recreation Centre hours: 9:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
